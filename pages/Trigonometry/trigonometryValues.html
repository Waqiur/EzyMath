Values of some T−Ratios for many angles

  1. \[ \sin(7.5^{\circ})= \frac{\sqrt{2-\sqrt{2+\sqrt{3}}}}{2}= \cos(82.5^{\circ})= \sin \frac{\pi}{24} \]

  2. \[ \cos(7.5^{\circ})= \frac{\sqrt{2+\sqrt{2+\sqrt{3}}}}{2}= \sin(82.5^{\circ})= \cos \frac{\pi}{24} \]

  3. \[ \tan(7.5^{\circ})= \sqrt{6}-\sqrt{3}+\sqrt{2}-2=(\sqrt{2}-1)(\sqrt{3}-\sqrt{2})= \cot(82.5^{\circ})= \tan\frac{\pi}{24} \]

  4. \[ \cot(7.5^{\circ})= \sqrt{6}+\sqrt{3}+\sqrt{2}+2=(\sqrt{2}+1)(\sqrt{3}+\sqrt{2})= \tan(82.5^{\circ})= \cot\frac{\pi}{24} \]

  5. \[ \sin(15^{\circ})= \frac{\sqrt{3}-1}{2\sqrt{2}}= \cos(75^{\circ})= \sin \frac{\pi}{12} \]

  6. \[ \cos(15^{\circ})= \frac{\sqrt{3}+1}{2\sqrt{2}}= \sin(75^{\circ})= \cos \frac{\pi}{12} \]

  7. \[ \tan(15^{\circ})= 2-\sqrt{3}= \cot(75^{\circ})= \tan\frac{\pi}{12} \]

  8. \[ \cot(15^{\circ})= 2+\sqrt{3}= \tan(75^{\circ})= \cot\frac{\pi}{12} \]

  9. \[ \sin(18^{\circ})= \frac{\sqrt{5}-1}{4}= \sqrt{\frac{3-\sqrt{5}}{8}} = \cos(72^{\circ})= \sin\frac{\pi}{10} \]

  10. \[ \cos(18^{\circ})= \frac{\sqrt{10+2\sqrt{5}}}{4}= \sqrt{\frac{5+\sqrt{5}}{8}} = \sin(72^{\circ})= \cos\frac{\pi}{10} \]

  11. \[ \tan(18^{\circ})= \sqrt{1-\frac{2\sqrt{5}}{5}}= \cot(72^{\circ})= \tan\frac{\pi}{10} \]

  12. \[ \cot(18^{\circ})= \sqrt{5+2\sqrt{5}}= \tan(72^{\circ})= \cot\frac{\pi}{10} \]

  13. \[ \sin(22.5^{\circ})= \frac{\sqrt{2-\sqrt{2}}}{2}= \sqrt{\frac{4-\sqrt{8}}{8}} = \cos(67.5^{\circ})= \sin\frac{\pi}{8} \]

  14. \[ \cos(22.5^{\circ})= \frac{\sqrt{2+\sqrt{2}}}{2}= \sqrt{\frac{4+\sqrt{8}}{8}} = \sin(67.5^{\circ})= \cos\frac{\pi}{8} \]

  15. \[ \tan(22.5^{\circ})=\sqrt{2}-1=\cot(67.5^{\circ})= \tan\frac{\pi}{8} \]

  16. \[ \cot(22.5^{\circ})=1+\sqrt{2}=\tan(67.5^{\circ})= \cot\frac{\pi}{8} \]

  17. \[ \sin(36^{\circ})= \frac{\sqrt{10-2\sqrt{5}}}{4}= \sqrt{\frac{5-\sqrt{5}}{8}}= \cos(54^{\circ})= \sin\frac{\pi}{5} \]

  18. \[ \cos(36^{\circ})= \frac{\sqrt{5}+1}{4}= \sqrt{\frac{3+\sqrt{5}}{8}}= \sin(54^{\circ})= \cos \frac{\pi}{5} \]

  19. \[ \tan(36^{\circ})= \sqrt{5-2\sqrt{5}}= \cot(54^{\circ})= \tan \frac{\pi}{5} \]

  20. \[ \cot(36^{\circ})= \sqrt{\frac{5+2\sqrt{5}}{5}}= \tan(54^{\circ})= \cot \frac{\pi}{5} \]

  21. \[ \sin(37.5^{\circ})= \frac{\sqrt{2-\sqrt{2-\sqrt{3}}}}{2}= \cos(52.5^{\circ})= \sin \frac{5\pi}{24} \]

  22. \[ \cos(37.5^{\circ})= \frac{\sqrt{2+\sqrt{2-\sqrt{3}}}}{2}= \sin(52.5^{\circ})= \cos \frac{5\pi}{24} \]

  23. \[ \tan(37.5^{\circ})= \sqrt{6}+\sqrt{3}-\sqrt{2}-2=(\sqrt{2}+1)(\sqrt{3}-\sqrt{2})= \cot(52.5^{\circ})= \tan\frac{5\pi}{24} \]

  24. \[ \cot(37.5^{\circ})= \sqrt{6}-\sqrt{3}-\sqrt{2}+2=(\sqrt{2}-1)(\sqrt{3}+\sqrt{2})= \tan(52.5^{\circ})= \cot\frac{5\pi}{24} \]
